Frequently Asked Questions

Q What is TaskMaster?

TaskMaster is an AI-powered assistant that lets you create automated tasks, schedule them, and receive clean reports delivered right on time. Think of it as your personal agent that works in the background.

Q How do I create a task?

Open the app, tap the "+" button on the Tasks screen, describe what you want your AI agent to do, set a schedule, and you're all set. Your agent handles the rest.

Q Is TaskMaster free to use?

TaskMaster is free to download with limited features. To unlock the full power of your AI assistant, you can subscribe to our premium plan.

Q How do I manage or cancel my subscription?

Go to your iPhone's Settings → Apple ID → Subscriptions, then select TaskMaster to manage or cancel. You can also manage your subscription from within the app under Settings → Manage Subscription.

Q The app is crashing. What should I do?

First, make sure you're running the latest version of TaskMaster from the App Store. Try restarting your device. If the issue persists, email us at support@documar.com with a description of what happened and your device model.

Q How do I delete my account?

Open the app, go to Settings → Delete Account. This action is permanent and will remove all your data, tasks, and reports from our servers.

Q Can I change my AI agent's name?

Yes, the agent is fully yours to customize.
