Privacy Policy

TaskMaster: AI Assistant · Effective March 31, 2026

Adem Tengiz ("we", "us", "our") operates the TaskMaster: AI Assistant mobile application. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use our app.

By creating an account and using TaskMaster, you agree to the collection and use of information as described in this policy.

Data Collection

Information We Collect

When you create an account and use TaskMaster, we collect the following information:

  • Name — used for account identification and personalisation
  • Email address — used for account creation, authentication, and communication
  • User-generated content — tasks, prompts, schedules, and AI-generated reports you create within the app
  • User ID & Device ID — used for account identification and app functionality
  • Purchase history — used to manage your subscription status

Data Usage

How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve the TaskMaster app and its features
  • Process your tasks and deliver AI-generated reports
  • Manage your account and subscription
  • Communicate with you about updates, support, and service-related matters
  • Detect and prevent fraud or abuse

Third-Party Services

Third-Party Services

TaskMaster uses the Google Gemini API to process your tasks and generate AI-powered reports. When you create or run a task, your prompts and task content may be sent to Google's servers for processing.

Please review Google's Privacy Policy for information on how Google handles data.

Subscription payments are processed by Apple through the App Store. We do not collect or store your payment card details. All payment information is handled directly by Apple.

Storage & Security

Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored on secure cloud servers. We use industry-standard security measures, including encryption in transit and at rest, to protect your personal information.

While we strive to protect your data, no method of electronic storage or transmission is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security.

Your Rights

Your Rights & Choices

You have the right to:

  • Delete your account — you can permanently delete your account and all associated data directly within the app at any time from Settings
  • Request your data — contact us to request a copy of the personal data we hold about you
  • Update your information — you can update your account details within the app

When you delete your account, all your data including tasks, reports, and personal information is permanently removed from our servers.

Children's Privacy

Children's Privacy

TaskMaster is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware that a child under 13 has provided us with personal data, we will take steps to delete that information promptly.

Updates

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we make significant changes, we will notify you through the app or via email. We encourage you to review this page periodically.
